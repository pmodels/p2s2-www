Submission Instructions

Submissions should be in PDF format in U.S. letter size paper, and should be formatted in a double-column format with a font size 10 pt or larger. They should not exceed 10 pages (all inclusive). Please follow the ACM format located at https://www.acm.org/publications/proceedings-template . Submissions will be judged based on relevance, significance, originality, correctness and clarity.

The paper submission online system is open: https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=p2s22018

Journal Special Issue

The P2S2-2021 workshop papers will be invited to extend the manuscripts to be considered for a Special Issue on Parallel Programming Models and Systems Software for High-End Computing of the Concurrency and Computation: Practice and Experience (CCPE), edited by John Leidel and Min Si. This special issue is dedicated for the papers accepted in the P2S2 workshop. The submission to this special issue is by invitation only.

The following lists the special issues published in the past.
